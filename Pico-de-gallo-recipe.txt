Ingredients:
 - 3 yellow or red onions
 - 12 Roma tomatoes
 - 2 cups fresh cilantro leaves
 - 2 to 3 jalapenos
 - 1 lime
 - Salt
 
Directions:
 1. Dice up equal quantities of onion and tomato.
 2. Roughly chop the cilantro.
 3. Slice 1 or 2 jalapenos in half and scrape out the seeds with a spoon.
 4. Dice the jalapenos very finely. Put the four ingredients in a bowl.
 5. Slice the lime in half and squeeze the juice from half a lime the bowl.
 6. Sprinkle with salt, and stir together until combined.
